About us

Paris week festival held annually in the picturesque town, attracts thousands of visitors from all over the world. The atmosphere is charged with positive energy and joy as people come together to celebrate culture, art and music. The main stage hosts well-known bands that make the crowds dance with their catchy tunes, while smaller stages showcase local talent and alternative genres. Alongside the music, visitors can enjoy a variety of gastronomic stalls offering local specialities and international dishes. There are creative workshops and fun activities for children, so the festival really is for the whole family. The art installations and exhibitions that complete the space are thought-provoking and inspire new ways of looking at the world. At night, the area transforms into a magical realm of lights and colours as people dance and share unforgettable moments under the stars. This festival is not just about music, it's a celebration of life and community that brings people of different generations and cultures together.

Singers

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter who is known for her unique style combining pop, country and indie. She was born on 13 December 1989 in Reading, Pennsylvania and began her career as a teenager. She is known not only for her catchy melodies, but also for her personal and emotional lyrics, which often reflect her life experiences and relationships. Taylor is a multiple Grammy winner and her album “Folklore” was a huge success, showing her artistic diversity

Katy Perry

Katy Perry, by her stage name Katheryn Elizabeth Hudson, is an American pop singer and songwriter who was born on October 25, 1984 in Santa Barbara, California. She is known for her distinctive voice, catchy melodies and extravagant style. Her career took off after the release of the hit single “I Kissed a Girl” in 2008, which made her famous worldwide. Other successful tracks such as “Firework”, “Teenage Dream” and “Roar” followed. Katy is also known for her live performances and colorful music videos.
