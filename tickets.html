Tickets

ULTRA PARIS FESTIVAL

Don't know which ticket will be the best for you?
Below you will find a clear description of the individual categories, including everything they contain.

Tickets for the Ultra Paris Festival are available in 3 categories with different levels of benefits.

Classic Ticket

Basic ticket for the entire festival.

99 €

  • Full festival ticket
  • Entrance to the premises during opening hours

Ticket+

Upgraded ticket for the entire festival.

149 €

  • Full festival ticket
  • Entrance to the premises during opening hours
  • Free paid toilets in the entire complex
  • After party until 4 AM

VIP Ticket

VIP ticket for the entire festival.

199 €

  • Full festival ticket
  • Entrance to the premises during opening hours
  • Free paid toilets in the entire complex
  • After party until 4 AM
  • Fast track entrance to the premises
  • VIP parking
